Positions (A–I)

  • A — Past, beginning of the situation, or inertia: what is keeping the situation blocked and stagnant.
  • B — Present: development of conditions about which clarification is requested; how to observe the current movement. Advice of the dead is given for this card.
  • C — Future: the conclusion of the entire situation or the balance that will be achieved.
  • D — Advantages: strong points to use and preserve, what comes to our aid, active influences.
  • E — Disadvantages: weak points to be careful of, what to remove ourselves from, risks.
  • F (Chalices) — Emotional center: feelings, influence of memories.
  • G (Pentacles) — Material center: economic situation, appearance, physical health.
  • H (Swords) — Mental center: thoughts, ideas, communication.
  • I (Wands) — Creative center: desires, expression and creativity.

Unlock with a gift of 25k+ coins (e.g. Lion/Universe) or type !equilibrium in chat. Only the gift sender may ask the question.
