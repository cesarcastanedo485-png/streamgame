# Santa Muerte Equilibrium — Custom Reading Method

## The five decks

Divide the standard tarot into five parts, keeping the Major Arcana separate from the Minor Arcana, and the Minor Arcana divided into separate suits. Five decks are created:

1. **Deck one:** The twenty-two Major Arcana  
2. **Deck two:** The fourteen arcana of the Suit of Chalices  
3. **Deck three:** The fourteen arcana of the Suit of Pentacles  
4. **Deck four:** The fourteen arcana of the Suit of Swords  
5. **Deck five:** The fourteen arcana of the Suit of Wands  

Keep each deck separate. Shuffle each deck separately before drawing.

---

## Formulate and visualize the question

Formulate and visualize the question relating to the reading before drawing. The question should reflect what the querent wishes to clarify.

---

## Drawing and placement

### From deck one (Major Arcana)

Take the twenty-two Major Arcana, mix the cards, and extract five cards. Place them as illustrated in the grid:

- **Row 3 (bottom):** A (left), B (center), C (right)  
- **Row 1 (top):** D (left), E (right)  

### From the four Minor decks

Take the other four decks (Chalices, Pentacles, Swords, Wands) and mix each separately. Extract one card from each deck. These cards provide ulterior, more in-depth information regarding the balance and the state of our centers—emotive, material, intellectual, creative—so we can see how much these centers are involved in our actual state and what aspects of our personality, body, or environment have a correct amount of energy, which state is neglected, and which we need to balance to reach a healthy equilibrium.

Place them as illustrated:

- **Row 2 (middle):** F (above A), G and H (side by side above B), I (above C)

---

## Position meanings (A–I)

### A — Bottom left  
This card represents **the past**, the beginning of the situation the querent wishes to clarify, or else it represents **inertia**: what is keeping the situation blocked and stagnant.

### B — Next to A, to the right (center bottom)  
This card identifies **the present situation**, the development of conditions about which a request for clarification is made, and it provides information regarding how to observe the current movement.

### C — Next to B, to the right (bottom right)  
This card represents **the future**, the conclusion of the entire situation or the balance that will be achieved.

### D — Top row, all the way to the left  
This card represents **what is to our advantage**: our strong points to use and to preserve, plus that which comes to our aid or, more generally, the **active influences**.

### E — Top row, all the way to the right  
This card represents **what is to our disadvantage**: the weak points which we must be careful of and which we must remove ourselves from, the risk or **receptive influences** with which we can develop a new point of view.

### F — Row two, directly above A  
Extracted from the deck of the **Suit of Chalices**. It represents the state of our **emotional center**, our feelings, and also the influence of our memories.

### G — Row two, directly next to F to the right, above B  
Extracted from the deck of the **Minor Arcana of Pentacles**. It represents the state of our **material center**: how much is influencing our economic situation at this moment, our appearance, and our physical health.

### H — Row two, directly next to G, above B (so that two cards are directly above B, side by side)  
Extracted from the deck of the **Minor Arcana of Swords**. It represents the state of our **mental center**: our thoughts, our intellectual activity, our ideas, and how we manage to communicate with the external world.

### I — Row two, all the way to the right, above C  
Extracted from the deck of the **Minor Arcana of Wands**. It represents the state of our **creative center**: the situation of our desires, our sexual satisfaction, and also how we manage to express and channel creativity.

---

## Closure: advice of the dead

At the closure of the reading, consult **the advice of the dead** relating to **card B** (the present). Card B refers to the present instance; the advice of the dead offers a suggestion as to how to act and project oneself towards a better future.
